Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: 11 May 2026

Welcome to All Pin Service. This Refund and Cancellation Policy explains the terms under which payments made on the All Pin Service mobile application are processed, refunded, or cancelled.

By using our application and making payments through the platform, you agree to this policy.

1. Nature of Services

All Pin Service is a digital platform that allows service providers to register and list their services for customers. Registration and listing services may require payment of a non-refundable onboarding or registration fee.

2. Registration Fees

Any registration, onboarding, subscription, promotional, or listing fee paid by service providers on the All Pin Service platform is generally non-refundable once the payment has been successfully processed.

This is because digital onboarding, verification, moderation, and listing-related services begin immediately after successful payment.

3. Duplicate Payments

In case a user or service provider is charged more than once for the same transaction due to technical issues, the extra charged amount may be refunded after verification.

To request a duplicate payment refund, users must contact support within 7 days of the transaction date with:

  • Registered mobile number
  • Transaction ID
  • Payment screenshot or proof (if available)

After verification, eligible refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

4. Failed Transactions

If a payment is deducted from the user's bank account but the transaction status on All Pin Service shows failed or pending:

  • The amount is usually automatically reversed by the payment provider or bank within 5–7 business days.
  • If reversal does not happen within this period, users may contact our support team.

5. Cancellation Policy

Once a service provider registration or listing request is submitted and payment is completed, cancellation requests may not be accepted.

However, All Pin Service reserves the right to review cancellation requests on a case-by-case basis.

6. Platform Rights

All Pin Service reserves the right to:

  • Reject or remove service provider listings that violate platform policies
  • Suspend accounts involved in fraudulent or suspicious activities
  • Refuse refund requests in cases of policy violations or misuse of services
